WHITE Azores

A dozen meters from the sea-facing south, WHITE is the result of the passion that João and Catarina Reis share for the art of creating unique places in unique environments. The aim is to provide an exclusive experience to those who visit us, where time has another rhythm that brings us the stillness of the Azorean nature.

It was during a walk that João and Catarina Reis identified the ruins of an old summer house, where the immensity of the views and the magic of the place made them dream, this dream became a reality with WHITE.

With only 9 suites and 2 Villas, all types of accommodation offer sea views (except LA MAISON).

Junior Suites

  • Featuring a contemporary design, the Junior Suites are harmoniously integrated into the building and glamorous atmosphere of WHITE. These Suites are located on the ground floor and have direct access to the outdoor spaces and to the swimming pool – a mirror of the Atlantic sitting right in front of you.

Unique Suites

  • Located on the ground floor, the Unique Suites occupy unique places where some remains of the history of the manor house are preserved. Stone arches framing the ceiling of the old wine cellar, walls decorated with basalt stone and the fireplace of the old kitchen nestled in large stone slabs are some of the elements that identify a bygone era.

Ocean Suites

The infinite blue that can be seen from the windows of the four Ocean Suites is nothing short of splendid. Located on the 1st floor, they afford the best view from WHITE. The Ocean Suites offer suspended balconies and reading corners overlooking the ocean - a real treat for contemplation and relaxation.

The Villa

The Villa is the most reserved nook of WHITE, which is characterized by its cliff-top location and its exclusive heated pool, providing guests unparalleled ocean views. An authentic retreat, The Villa is the perfect place for a few days of rest in an almost secret place.

La Maison

Reserved and spacious, La Maison is located 50 meters from the Hotel and boasts 400 m2 of area with 4 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms, dining room, living room, heated outdoor pool, croquet court and kids playground. It is the perfect choice for larger families or for a group of friends looking for a unique place with 5* hotel services.

Wellness & Fitness

Enjoy moments of pure wellness at WHITE, where you will find the natural ingredients to unwind from everyday worries. Our pergola is carefully designed to give you the rest you deserve with a privileged view of the cliff with the tranquil sound of the sea as background music.
